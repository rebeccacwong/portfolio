URL Media Templates

I worked with URL Media, a media startup that is centered on creating sustainability for Black and Brown-owned local media outlets, in 2022 to design various Instagram social media templates for their usage.

Per the client's request, the templates were created in Canva, allowing them to easily edit the templates to produce content. I created some of the assets for the template in Adobe Illustrator and Adobe After Effects, and imported the content to Canva. The designs were based off of their existing brand identity at the time, utilizing their brand's color palette and font.
