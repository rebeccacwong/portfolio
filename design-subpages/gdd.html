You are the Shopkeeper in an RPG (game)

You Are the Shopkeeper in an RPG is a game that I designed and developed in a team of 4 in 9 weeks. We created the game using Unity and it can be played here.

The objective of the game is to make as much money as possible by selling items to customers in your store.

I primarily took on an artist role, but contributed to the code when necessary. I also created sound effects and one of the game songs from scratch.
