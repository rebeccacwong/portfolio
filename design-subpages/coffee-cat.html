Coffee Cat

Coffee Cat is a 3d animated short that I created in a group of 4 for UCBUGG (UC Berkeley Undergraduate Graphics Group) 3d modeling and animation course. We created the short over the course of a semester in Autodesk Maya.

My primary contributions included modeling/shading/skinning/rigging the kittens, animating the beginning shots as the cat approaches the shaky bridge, and lastly compositing and editing the entire short in Adobe After Effects.
