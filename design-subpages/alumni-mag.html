UC Berkeley School of Information: Alumni Magazine

At the end of each year, the UC Berkeley School of Information sends out an alumni magazine with notable stories shared, spotlights on specific alumni, and news with changes in the school.

I annually lead the entire visual design of the magazine, planning out a visual theme (that is in-line with school branding) and designing each spread. On the left are a few of the spreads from the 2020 and 2021 issue.
